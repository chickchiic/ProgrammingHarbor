About Us

After years of continuous research and innovation, Harbor Technology has integrated AI algorithms into its noise reduction technology, which processes the vibration frequency using acoustic audio processing technology, and combined it with a self-developed broadband low-noise vibration sensor. We have launched the new generation of industry-leading AI Diagnostic and Prognostic Solution (AIDPS), which solves the problem of difficult vibration frequency detection and vibration model prediction in complex environments, and is an indispensable solution for industries to accelerate intelligence.
